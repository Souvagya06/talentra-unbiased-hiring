expand_more

  • Candidate resume metadata for bias analysis.

expand_more

Information is strictly used to generate Talentra Insights. We never sell data to third parties. Data is used to train our proprietary debiasing models in a strictly anonymized and aggregated manner.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Talentra ensure unbiased hiring? expand_more
Our AI uses natural language processing to identify and strip out demographic indicators from evaluations, focusing purely on skill-based performance indicators and sentiment analysis of structured responses.
Is my data secure? expand_more
Yes, we use bank-grade encryption and follow strict compliance standards including SOC2 and GDPR. Your data is your own, and we provide tools to export or purge it at any time.
Can I download reports? expand_more
Absolutely. All analysis, including candidate comparison sheets and diversity impact reports, can be exported as high-resolution PDF or CSV files.
How does AI analyze interviews? expand_more
The AI transcribes audio/video input and scores candidate answers based on pre-defined competency frameworks, while checking for consistency and highlighting key professional insights.
Is communication score used in hiring decisions? expand_more
Communication score is provided as an auxiliary metric for roles that require high interpersonal skills. It is not weighted heavily by default to prevent cultural bias, but can be customized per role.
